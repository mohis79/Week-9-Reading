✅ Book Expectations for Every Reading Task

  1. Write the full date and title.
  2. Underline the date and title neatly with a ruler.
  3. Before reading, write a prediction: I think this text will be about… because…
  4. Write 5 unfamiliar or interesting vocabulary words.
  5. Copy each question first.
  6. Answer underneath in full sentences.
  7. Use evidence from the text where you can.

🧠 Sentence Starters

  • I predict… because…
  • The character felt… because…
  • One piece of evidence is…
  • This shows…
  • This reminds me of… because…

🧭 Daily Mission Steps

Tuesday 1 period

Open your first text/task. Write your date and title, make a prediction, list vocabulary, and begin reading with focus.

Wednesday 1 period

Continue your first text/task. Copy each question first, answer in full sentences, and underline one piece of evidence.

Thursday double lesson

Start your second text/task. Use your group link, read carefully, answer questions, and check the instructions before asking for help.

Friday 1 period

Finish all reading questions. Write a 4-sentence summary and complete a neat boxed visual sketch of an important moment from your text.
